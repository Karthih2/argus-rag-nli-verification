I would like to take this opportunity to point to an absolute gold mine for researchers in this topic, which is a Chinese-language book published in 2014 by the Communications University of China Press. He Bing and He Feng’s The Stories of Animation Outsourcing in China is a fascinating collection of testimonials about this topic, and is often our sole source in confirming the Chinese labor component to be found in many famous anime, including Sakura Wars, Banner of the Stars, Lodoss War, MADLAX, Cowboy Bebop, GTO and Death Note.
