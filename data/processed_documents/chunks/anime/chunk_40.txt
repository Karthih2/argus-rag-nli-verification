4, 2024, as part of their Intellectual Property Promotion Plan 2024, the Intellectual Property Strategy Headquarters announced their New Cool Japan Strategy in which content industries such as anime, manga, and video games were positioned as key industries. The New Cool Japan Strategy set the target figure of raising international content-related businesses to 20 trillion yen by 2033. The current international content market scale is 4.7 trillion yen (4.67 in 2022), of which anime accounts for 31% at 1.45 trillion yen. However, that ratio means it must reach roughly 6.2 trillion yen by 2033. The 2023 international market is 1.7 trillion yen, so there is a 4.78 trillion yen difference.
