I told him to look at the credits, for companies like “Kaze Animation”, which is actually an incorrect transliteration of the Chinese name Feng Donghua, or the companies Hangzhou Donghua and Feilong Donghua (the latter sometimes transliterated into Japanese as “Hiryū Dōga” in translated credits), both set up by the Japanese animator Kaneko Katsunori. Sometimes these are just a single-line credit in a mass of text, standing in for literally hundreds of Chinese laborers, many of whom did not even know the title of the animation they were working on.
