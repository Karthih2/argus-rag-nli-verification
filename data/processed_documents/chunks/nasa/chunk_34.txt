Trends in the Anime Industry Market (2023) 〈The Anime Industry Market Greatly Exceeded 3 Trillion Yen, Reaching 114.3% of the Previous Year at 3 Trillion 465 Billion Yen〉 In 2023, the anime industry market once again broke its record high by reaching 114.3% of the previous year at 3.5 trillion yen, 418 billion yen more than the previous year. That growth rate and amount of increase is the highest ever. The anime industry market in 2002 was 1.96 trillion yen and it took 16 years to break 2 trillion yen in 2017, but this time it took only 7 years to break 3 trillion yen. It is proof that the market is still accelerating. These are the significant factors of the accelerated market, keeping in mind the circumstances of this growth.
