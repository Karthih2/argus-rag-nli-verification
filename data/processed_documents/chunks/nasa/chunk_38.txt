Overtook the Domestic Market〉 In 2023, the international market overtook the domestic market. This is the second time in history this has happened. The first time happened in 2020, and it already seemed like the international market would exceed the domestic market. Domestically, there were the fixed ideas that the Japanese market would grow sluggish due to the declining birth rate and population decrease, but it exceeded the growing international market in 2021 and 2022 due to the COVID-19 pandemic. However, in 2023, the domestic market broke its own record for the third year in a row by reaching 1.6 trillion yen (110.6% of the previous year, which was an increase of 155.8 billion yen).
