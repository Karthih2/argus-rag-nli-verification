The goal of these films has been plainly stated, with Miyazaki Hayao saying in interviews that his intention in making his animations is to tell children that ‘it’s good to be alive’ 51. This implies that the formal techniques used to create this spectacular mundane are intended to reassure the young target audience that conte ntment can be found in everyday life, not only in the fantastic spectacle that Ghibli depicts as its equal.
